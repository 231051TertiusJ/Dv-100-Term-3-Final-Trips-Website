More About us

On our very own trips page you'll see trips spanning from Tortuga to the old Spanish naval forts. Its a place you must visit once in your life!
Sail the great Caribbean seas where pirates roamed and explores made history. Our trips take you through the historical seas and give you a chance to get face to face with the same sceneries pirates like Blackbeard saw during the pirate era.

Barbados: A tropical paradise renowned for its pristine beaches, Barbados offers visitors a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. Explore the historic capital of Bridgetown, indulge in water sports, or discover the island's vibrant cultural heritage.

Jamaica: Known for its reggae music and laid-back atmosphere, Jamaica captivates travelers with its stunning beaches, lush mountains, and warm hospitality. Immerse yourself in the vibrant markets, visit the birthplace of Bob Marley, or savor mouthwatering local cuisine.

Puerto Rico: As a U.S. territory, Puerto Rico offers a unique blend of American comforts and Caribbean charm. From the cobblestone streets of Old San Juan to the breathtaking El Yunque rainforest, this island is a diverse destination rich in history and natural beauty.

St. Lucia: Blessed with breathtaking landscapes, St. Lucia is a haven for nature enthusiasts. The majestic Pitons, volcanic peaks rising dramatically from the sea, provide a stunning backdrop for hiking, while the island's coral reefs and clear turquoise waters are ideal for diving and snorkeling.

Bahamas: Comprising over 700 islands and cays, the Bahamas is a dreamy archipelago boasting pristine white-sand beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Nassau, the vibrant capital, offers a unique blend of colonial architecture, bustling markets, and luxurious resorts.

Cayman Islands: Renowned for its world-class diving spots, the Cayman Islands offer an underwater paradise teeming with colorful coral reefs and diverse marine life. Grand Cayman, the largest island, also offers upscale shopping, fine dining, and a lively nightlife scene.

Aruba: Located on the southern fringe of the Caribbean Sea, Aruba promises visitors a sun-soaked escape with its beautiful beaches, constant trade winds, and friendly atmosphere. Explore the vibrant capital of Oranjestad, experience thrilling water sports, or relax in a luxurious resort.

Turks and Caicos: With its powder-soft white-sand beaches and crystal-clear waters, the Turks and Caicos Islands are a haven for beach lovers and water enthusiasts. Providenciales, the main island, offers world-class snorkeling, diving, and luxurious resorts along Grace Bay Beach.

These incredible destinations within the Caribbean Sea promise unforgettable experiences, with a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and relaxation

Diverse Shark Species: The Caribbean is home to around 50 species of sharks, including the nurse shark, lemon shark, Caribbean reef shark, and tiger shark.

Nurse Sharks: Nurse sharks are commonly found in the Caribbean. They are bottom-dwelling sharks and are relatively harmless to humans, preferring to feed on small fish and crustaceans.

Caribbean Reef Sharks: The Caribbean reef shark is a common sight in Caribbean waters. These sharks are known for their sleek appearance and social behavior, often forming groups or "shivers" near coral reefs.

Apex Predators: Sharks play a crucial role in maintaining a healthy marine ecosystem. As apex predators, they help balance the population of smaller marine species and promote biodiversity.

Migratory Routes: Several shark species, such as the tiger shark and bull shark, migrate through Caribbean waters. These migrations can cover great distances and often lead to encounters with other marine species.

Jaws of the Tiger: The Caribbean is known for its tiger shark population. These sharks have a unique ability to consume a wide range of prey, leading to their nickname "the garbage cans of the sea."
